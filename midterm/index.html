Welcome to Installation 04

In the far off future of 2552 AD, lies a giant ring shaped object located in the Orion Arm of the Milky Way Galaxy that has existed since it's construction in 97,445 BCE. This object is known as Installation 04 also known as Alpha Halo.

Structure

The size of Alpha Halo has a complete diameter of roughly 10,000kms similar to that of Earth. Despite it's ringed shape, Alpha Halo supports breathable air and a multitude of unique biomes capaple of supporting life. Because of the wide variety of habitats that Alpha Halo exibits this means that there are a great many tourist destinations that you can visit during your vacation stay at Installation 04!
